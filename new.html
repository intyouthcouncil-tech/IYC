About Us

Who We Are

We are a youth-led governance institution committed to developing the next generation of leaders, policymakers, and innovators through structured programs, research initiatives, and diplomatic engagement.

Our Governance Structure

Operating as a formal youth think tank, our council maintains democratic processes, executive leadership, and specialized committees. We combine academic rigor with practical policy development to create measurable impact.
